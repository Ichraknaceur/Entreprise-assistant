# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Purpose
This policy outlines the rules for reimbursement of business-related expenses.

## Eligible Expenses
Eligible expenses include approved travel, accommodation, meals during business trips, conference registration fees, and local transport. All expenses must be necessary, reasonable, and directly related to company business.

## Submission Process
Expense reports must be submitted through the finance platform within 15 calendar days after the expense date. Original receipts or valid digital copies are mandatory.

## Approval Workflow
The employee’s manager must approve the expense before reimbursement is processed by Finance.

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses
Personal expenses, alcohol outside client events, late fees, traffic fines, and undocumented expenses are not reimbursable.

## Travel Policy Reference
Business travel must follow the separate corporate travel policy regarding booking class, hotel range, and transport limits.

## Reimbursement Timeline
Approved expense reports are reimbursed during the next payroll cycle or within 30 days depending on the payment method.
