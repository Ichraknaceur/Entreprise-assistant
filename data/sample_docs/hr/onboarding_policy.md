# Employee Onboarding Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines the onboarding process for new employees to ensure smooth integration, secure access setup, and compliance with company standards.

## Scope
This policy applies to all permanent employees, contractors, and interns joining the company.

## Preboarding
Before the employee’s first day, the hiring manager must submit the onboarding request form at least five business days in advance. HR verifies employment documents and confirms the start date. IT prepares the laptop, company email account, and access credentials.

## First Day Activities
On the first day, the employee attends an HR welcome session covering company values, internal policies, and administrative procedures. The manager introduces the employee to the team and reviews the 30-day onboarding plan.

## Mandatory Access and Equipment
Each employee receives a company laptop, secure VPN access, collaboration tool accounts, and email credentials. Access to internal applications is granted based on role approval.

## Training Requirements
New employees must complete mandatory training on security awareness, data protection, code of conduct, and remote work guidelines within the first two weeks.

## Probation Follow-up
Managers must schedule onboarding check-ins after 1 week, 1 month, and 3 months to ensure successful integration.
