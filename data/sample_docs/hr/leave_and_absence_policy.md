# Leave and Absence Policy

## Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to annual paid leave according to local labor regulations and employment contracts. Leave requests must be submitted through the HR system at least two weeks in advance whenever possible.

## Sick Leave
Employees must notify their manager as soon as possible when unable to work due to illness. A medical certificate may be required after a defined number of absence days depending on local rules.

## Exceptional Leave
Exceptional leave may be granted for family events, bereavement, or other significant personal circumstances, subject to HR validation.

## Unpaid Leave
Unpaid leave may be granted in exceptional cases based on manager and HR approval.

## Leave Approval
All leave requests must be approved by the direct manager based on team workload, project priorities, and staffing constraints.

## Record Keeping
HR maintains official leave records. Employees are responsible for ensuring their requests are properly recorded in the HR platform.
