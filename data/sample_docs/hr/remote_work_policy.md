# Remote Work Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines the conditions under which employees may work remotely.

## Eligibility
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week, subject to manager approval and business needs. Some roles may require more on-site presence.

## Working Hours
Employees working remotely must remain available during core hours from 9:30 AM to 4:30 PM local time. Team-specific schedules may apply.

## Equipment
The company provides a laptop, charger, headset, and secure VPN access. Employees are responsible for maintaining a safe and quiet work environment.

## Security Requirements
Company data must only be accessed through corporate devices and secured connections. Sensitive documents must not be printed or stored on personal devices.

## Communication Expectations
Employees must remain reachable via approved communication tools and participate in scheduled meetings unless on approved leave.

## Exceptions
Requests for full remote work or long-term exceptions must be approved by HR and the employee’s department head.
