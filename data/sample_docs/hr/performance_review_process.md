# Performance Review Process

## Objective
The performance review process supports employee development, feedback quality, and alignment with company objectives.

## Review Frequency
Formal performance reviews are conducted twice a year. Informal feedback is encouraged continuously.

## Evaluation Criteria
Employees are assessed on role delivery, collaboration, communication, problem-solving, ownership, and alignment with company values.

## Review Steps
The process includes self-assessment, manager assessment, optional peer feedback, calibration, and final review discussion.

## Development Planning
Each review must include a development plan with learning goals, skill-building actions, and career aspirations.

## Documentation
Performance evaluations must be documented in the HR platform and kept confidential.

## Promotion Consideration
Promotion decisions are based on sustained performance, demonstrated impact, role readiness, and business context.
