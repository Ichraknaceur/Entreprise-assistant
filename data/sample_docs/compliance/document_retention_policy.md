# Document Retention Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines how long company documents must be retained and when they should be archived or deleted.

## Scope
It applies to HR files, financial records, contracts, project documentation, support records, and compliance materials.

## Retention Rules
Retention periods vary by document type and legal requirements. Business owners must ensure retention schedules are documented and followed.

## Archiving
Documents with ongoing business or legal value may be archived in approved systems with controlled access.

## Deletion
Documents that exceed retention requirements and have no ongoing legal or business value must be securely deleted.

## Legal Hold
If a legal hold applies, deletion must be suspended until the hold is lifted.

## Responsibilities
Document owners, compliance teams, and IT administrators share responsibility for proper retention and disposal.
