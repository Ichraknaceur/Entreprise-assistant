# GDPR Data Handling Policy

## Purpose
This policy ensures personal data is collected, processed, stored, and deleted in accordance with data protection requirements.

## Personal Data Definition
Personal data includes any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual, such as names, emails, phone numbers, identification numbers, or behavioral data.

## Processing Principles
Personal data must be processed lawfully, fairly, transparently, for a specific purpose, and only to the extent necessary.

## Access and Minimization
Only authorized personnel may access personal data, and only the minimum necessary data should be processed.

## Retention and Deletion
Personal data must not be kept longer than necessary. Data retention schedules must be defined and applied.

## Incident Reporting
Any personal data breach or suspected breach must be reported immediately to the Data Protection Officer and IT Security.

## Third Parties
Any transfer of personal data to third parties must be contractually justified and compliant with applicable legal requirements.
