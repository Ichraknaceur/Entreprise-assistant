# Vendor Security Review Process

## Purpose
This process defines how third-party vendors are evaluated before accessing company data or systems.

## Scope
It applies to SaaS providers, infrastructure vendors, outsourcing partners, and any external service handling company information.

## Review Criteria
Vendors are assessed on security certifications, encryption practices, access control, incident response, data hosting location, and compliance commitments.

## Risk Classification
Vendors are classified as low, medium, or high risk depending on the sensitivity of the services and data involved.

## Approval Steps
Business sponsor approval, procurement validation, and security review are required before contract signature for medium- and high-risk vendors.

## Ongoing Monitoring
Critical vendors may be reviewed annually or after major security incidents.
