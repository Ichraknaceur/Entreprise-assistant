# Access Control Policy

## Objective
This policy defines the principles governing access to systems, applications, and data.

## Least Privilege
Users must receive only the minimum access necessary to perform their job functions.

## Role-Based Access
Whenever possible, access should be assigned through predefined roles rather than individual permissions.

## Privileged Access
Administrative privileges must be restricted, logged, and reviewed regularly.

## Joiner, Mover, Leaver
Access rights must be granted, modified, or removed promptly when employees join, change roles, or leave the company.

## Periodic Reviews
Managers and system owners must review access rights periodically to detect excessive or outdated permissions.

## Auditability
All critical access changes must be recorded to support audit and compliance requirements.
