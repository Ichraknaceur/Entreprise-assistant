# Password and MFA Policy

## Password Rules
Passwords must contain at least 12 characters and include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Password reuse across systems is prohibited.

## MFA Requirements
Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for email, VPN, HR systems, cloud tools, and administrative applications.

## Password Storage
Passwords must be stored only in the approved enterprise password manager. Writing down passwords or sharing them through chat or email is forbidden.

## Compromised Credentials
Any suspected credential leak must be reported immediately to IT Security. The affected password must be changed without delay.

## Account Lockout
After repeated failed login attempts, accounts may be temporarily locked for security reasons.

## Exceptions
Temporary exceptions may be approved only by IT Security for technical migration cases.
