# Incident Management Process

## Purpose
This process ensures incidents affecting systems, services, or users are identified, prioritized, communicated, and resolved consistently.

## Incident Classification
Incidents are classified by severity:
- Critical: major business disruption
- High: significant degradation
- Medium: moderate service issue
- Low: limited user impact

## Reporting
Employees must report incidents through the IT service desk portal or hotline. Reports should include symptoms, timing, affected systems, and screenshots if relevant.

## Response Targets
Critical incidents require immediate triage and escalation. High incidents must be reviewed within one hour. Medium and Low incidents follow standard response windows.

## Communication
For major incidents, the incident manager coordinates stakeholder updates until resolution and closure.

## Post-Incident Review
Critical and High incidents require a post-mortem analysis documenting root cause, corrective actions, and preventive measures.
