# VPN Access Guide

## Purpose
This guide explains how employees can securely connect to internal company resources from outside the office network.

## Requirements
Users must have a company-managed laptop, an active company account, and multi-factor authentication enabled.

## Installation Steps
Install the approved VPN client from the company software portal. Log in using your corporate email and password, then complete MFA verification.

## Connection Rules
VPN must be enabled when accessing internal systems, shared drives, admin portals, or sensitive applications from external networks.

## Troubleshooting
If the VPN connection fails, check internet connectivity, verify MFA status, restart the client, and review whether your account is active. If issues persist, contact IT support.

## Security Reminder
Do not install unapproved VPN software or attempt to bypass the company VPN for internal access.
